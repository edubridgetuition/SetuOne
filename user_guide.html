SetuOne ERP - Comprehensive User Manual

Your step-by-step guide to navigate the Integrated Facility Operations platform from login to exit.

🔑 1. Getting Started & Authentication

SetuOne uses a role-based landing dashboard. To access specialized configurations, use the target demo accounts:

  • Manager (Admin Access): manager@orion.test (Password: password)
  • General Employee: employee@orion.test (Password: password)
  • 1
    Open Login Screen

    Enter the credentials matching your operational profile and click Sign In.

  • 2
    Dashboard Overview

    View real-time pending ticket counts, active staff logs, and system counters at a single glance.

🎫 2. Helpdesk & Tickets Lifecycle

Used to file facility maintenance complaints (e.g., faulty AC, plumbing leak).

  • 1
    Raise a New Ticket

    Click "Raise Ticket" button, specify Category, Subject details, exact building location, and Priority level.

  • 2
    Assign Ticket (Manager only)

    Managers can open any ticket and choose a staff assignee from the dropdown panel to start work.

  • 3
    Resolution Remarks

    Assignee transitions status to "Resolved" after adding mandatory diagnostic comments.

🖥️ 3. Asset Registry & AMC Contracts

Catalog physical devices and monitor Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC).

  • 1
    Register Asset

    Click "Register Asset", input Serial Number, Asset Category, purchase price, and location.

  • 2
    Assign or Transfer Assets

    Choose an asset from the list, click "Assign Asset" to choose an employee, or "Transfer" to re-route it.

  • 3
    Monitor AMC Expirations

    Navigate to "AMC Alert Panel" in the sidebar to review approaching vendor contract expiration dates.

🛒 4. Purchase Requisitions (Procurement)

Manage budget allocation and comparative vendor quote checks.

  • 1
    Raise PR Request

    Specify item requirements, estimated costs, and delivery timeline specifications.

  • 2
    Compare Quotations

    Upload files representing 3 separate vendor quotations. Compare details on the comparative grid.

  • 3
    Purchase Order Generation

    Choose the optimal quotation. The system automatically locks the deal and generates a Purchase Order (PO).

📦 5. Inventory & Consumption Tracking

Monitor daily consumables like office water bottles, returnable water jugs, and coffee machine materials.

  • 1
    Log Daily Water Bottles Delivery

    Select "Water Bottle Tracker", log incoming bottle counts, and specify returnable empty bottles count.

  • 2
    Coffee Material Logs

    Log monthly deliveries and usage stats for coffee machine coffee beans, milk, and cups.

🔧 6. Checklist Inspections & Preventive Maintenance

Prevent system breakdowns with Scheduled PPM Checklists.

  • 1
    Submit Daily Checklist

    Open the Checklist tab, inspect items (e.g. AC cooling, DG battery voltage) and submit PASS / FAIL updates.

  • 2
    Trigger Scheduled PPM

    Technicians check dynamic schedules, execute checks, and log spare parts consumed from stock.

👤 7. Visitor Gate Pass Control

Manage walk-in guests, issue passes, and capture security logs.

  • 1
    Check-in Guest

    Fill out the name, purpose dropdown, ID proof number, vehicle details, and click "Check-in" to generate pass `VIS-XXXXXX`.

  • 2
    Security Photo Capture

    Select the guest, trigger upload/webcam capture to link the face profile to the pass.

  • 3
    Check-out Guest

    Select the visitor, choose checkout remarks (e.g. Meeting Completed, No Show), and click "Stamps Out".

⏱️ 8. Geofenced Attendance Punching

Staff check-ins with GPS verification checks.

  • 1
    Check GPS geofence radar status

    Verify that the geofence indicator displays Green (Inside Office Radius).

  • 2
    Clock-in

    Select shift (General, Night, etc.), set method to GPS, and click "CLOCK IN".

  • 3
    Duty Status Toggle

    Switch between status flags (On Duty, Break, WFH) as needed. Click "CLOCK OUT" on departure.

  • 4
    Attendance Regularization

    If marked late, click "Regularize" on the history row and input justification details for manager approval.

📊 9. Dynamic Reports & Analytics Engine

Analyze performance metrics across all facility parameters on a dynamic comparative dashboard.

  • 1
    Interactive KPI Ribbon

    Click on any summary card (e.g. Open Tickets, Present Staff, Today Visitors) in the top ribbon to instantly drill down and load that specific report database view.

  • 2
    Dynamic Column Selection

    Check or uncheck column options (e.g. priority, status, date) to instantly show or hide that column in the live data table. Real-time rendering, no page refresh required!

  • 3
    Save Filters & Load Templates

    Enter a unique name for your active filters list, click "Save Filter". You can reload these specific settings at any time with a single click from the Saved Filters drawer.

  • 4
    Exporting Data

    Click the "Export CSV" button to download a spreadsheet-ready data log file directly to your device.

🤖 10. Notifications, Alerts & Automation Engine

Set up automated rules, configure custom email templates, and trigger notification channels dynamically.

  • 1
    Create Email Templates

    Navigate to the Email Templates tab. Add template variables (e.g. {{employee_name}}) inside the subject and body to automatically replace them when triggered.

  • 2
    Establish Recipient Groups

    Define groups like "Purchase Department" or "Facility Management" and map recipient emails to automatically route alert messages to multiple stakeholders.

  • 3
    Setup Active Automation Rules

    Set trigger thresholds (e.g. Alert if liters >= 5000). Map these rules to templates and recipient groups to automate system monitoring.

  • 4
    Trigger Queue Simulator

    Use the simulator sidebar to verify cron-jobs scheduler execution. Inspect latency (ms) and status in the live execution history terminal logs.
